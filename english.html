About me

My name is Leo Eriksson and I am a Web Designer

What I love about Web Design is the freedom to design and create and in the end providing a website that everyone can appreciate. When designing a website I want to provide an appropriate theme and feeling to the website in relation to the product. Because of this, and although I do have my preferences; my goal is to make each website both interactive and unique.

A great thing about Web Design is that it has almost endless learning, my school's education covers mostly front-end; which are aspects like design, pictures, buttons etc. In my free time I spend a lot of time educating myself on back-end with a goal to become a fullstack-developer. My next mission is learning safe web transactions and expanding this portfolio with more projects that I'm regularly working on.
